# Jamaican History Online

Jamaican History Online is a static digital archive project beginning with a searchable transcript record of Jamaican historical documents.

The first stage of the project is **Transcript Record**, a structured collection of transcribed wills, manumissions, deeds, estate papers, correspondence, parish records, and other historical sources relating to Jamaica.

## Project stages

- **00 — Transcript Record**
- **01 — People Index**
- **02 — Places Index**
- **03 — Topics and Themes**
- **04 — Essays and Research Notes**
- **05 — Maps**
- **06 — Family Networks**
- **07 — Source Guides**
- **08 — Recovered Archives**

## Editorial principles

Transcripts are prepared from historical records and may be corrected over time. Original spelling, capitalization, terminology, and historical language are generally preserved. Uncertain readings are marked clearly.

Images are not reproduced unless rights are clear. Where possible, transcript pages link back to the repository or source holding the original record.
